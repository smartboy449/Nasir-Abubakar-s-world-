My Story

My name is Nasir Abubakar. I am a 13-year-old boy from Kano, Nigeria with a big dream — to become a great engineer and build the fastest AI in the world. I believe in the power of learning, hard work, and helping others.

I started from humble beginnings, helping at home, studying daily, and dreaming big. I learned how to create websites and build technology step by step. Now, I'm building a special project called NasirBot, an AI helper to make people's lives easier.

My mission is not only to achieve my goals but also to inspire and support other children like me — especially those who don’t have many chances. One day, I want to create a global foundation to give hope, tools, and education to young dreamers.

This is only the beginning. I pray that my story touches hearts and opens doors for a better future for all of us. Insha Allah.
