My Story

I am Nasir Abubakar, a 13-year-old visionary from Kano, Nigeria. From a young age, I dreamed of using intelligence and coding to build tools that help people around the world.

Even with limited resources, I never gave up. I started learning how to design websites, code apps, and imagine artificial intelligence like NasirBot that can change lives.

My story is about hope, vision, and determination. I believe every child has the power to make a difference — and this is just the beginning of my journey.

My Goals:

  • ✨ Build the fastest and smartest AI (NasirBot)
  • ✨ Help others learn and grow
  • ✨ Show the world what kids from Africa can do

I am proud of my story, and I hope it inspires others to dream big and never give up.
