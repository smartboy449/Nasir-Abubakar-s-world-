My Story

My name is Nasir Abubakar. I am 13 years old and I live in Kano, Nigeria. I have a big dream — to become an engineer and build the fastest AI in the world. I am learning how to code, design websites, and use technology to help others.

I work hard every day, helping my family, praying, and staying focused on my goals. I believe that with knowledge, good intention, and teamwork, I can change the world. I created NasirBot, a chatbot to help people like ChatGPT, and I’m working on making it an app too.

My biggest hope is to inspire other kids like me — kids who are smart, kind, and full of dreams. I want to work with others around the world and make Muslims proud. One day, I will share my story with Elon Musk, OpenAI, and millions more. This is just the beginning.
